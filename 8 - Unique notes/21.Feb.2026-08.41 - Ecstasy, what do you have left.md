# Main contents
Ask yourself, would you, on why you are being here on this Earth. Was it to bust everything for ecstasy, instead of doing what you are supposed to do? After such ecstasy, what were there to be left, if it weren't the absence of meaning, the emptiness of the soul and the regret of your time. Why ought you delay what you need to to for longer it takes to do it. You don't value your time, nor do you have any respect for nature which existence, meaning and the mission it give you in this world.
