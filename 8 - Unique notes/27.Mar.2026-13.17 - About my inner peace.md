# Main contents
Idiotic I am, thinking that I would finally get some peace for my suffering in Pediatrics. How idiotic people are when they think one could learn anything while wasting their time. Such thoughts are rather obscure, unevidenced. And how obscure people must be when they think spending time performing meaningless, repetitive action in the hospital, without any concept about what the clinical situation might be theoretically, might actually convey a meaning or information in one's mind. Such obligation, it asserts no visible harm, yet worse, it is a waste of one's time, which is a precious resource, that one have limited, and which even money can't buy.
